About Us

A business boutique
that delivers results.

Auron is a boutique advisory specializing in the acquisition, sale, and structuring of high-value companies and assets.

We work on both sides of the table: we assist business owners looking to sell their companies in finding the right buyer at a fair price, and we connect investors and buyers to real opportunities with clear thesis and audited figures.

Our differentiator is technical rigor: every deal we present undergoes financial analysis, due diligence, and valuation before reaching the market. No speculation — only deals ready to close.

We leverage a proprietary platform with integrated AI for strategic analysis and asset matching — gaining speed and accuracy for corporations with acquisition appetite, connecting directly to the seller's interests and needs.

Local alternative investment solutions for global clients — with direct connections to major national and international investment funds.

Our Process

From first contact
to closing.

We don't sell illusions. Each step exists to protect seller and buyer and ensure a fair transaction.

01

Identification

We map the business or asset and understand the real motivation of the seller and the ideal buyer profile.

02

Due Diligence

Full analysis: financial, legal, operational, and tax. We uncover real risks and opportunities.

03

Valuation

Technical pricing via DCF and market multiples. The seller knows the value, the buyer knows what they are paying for.

04

Structuring

We set up the operation: governance, legal framework, investment thesis, and transition plan.

05

Connection

We connect qualified buyers and investors. We prepare Teasers and Infomemos to institutional standards.

06

Negotiation

We mediate the negotiation, protecting the interests of both sides until the agreement is reached.

07

Closing

We monitor the signing, transfer, and post-deal. Auron is present down to the last detail.
